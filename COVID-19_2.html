COVID-19

Information:

COVID-19 is a disease caused by a new strain of corona virus 'CO' stands for corona, 'VI' for virus and 'D' for disease. Formerly, this disease was referred to as '2019 novel coronavirus' or '2019-nCoV', COVID-19 is caused by infection with the severe acute respiratory system corona virus 2 (SARS-COV-2) virus strain.

Image:



Covid-19 Disease Effect:

  • Fever
  • Tiredness
  • Aches and Pains
  • Diarrhea
  • Headache
  • Loss of taste or smell
  • Sore throat
  • Myalgia
  • Encephalitis
  • Arthralgia
  • Conjunctivitis
  • Chest pain
  • Shortness of breath
  • Pneumonia
